Our Cleaning Checklist

What's included in Our Cleaning

We follow a detailed checklist to make sure every corner of your home is spotless.

Living Room

  • Dust all surfaces
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs
  • Sweep and mop floors
  • Wipe mirrors and windowsills
  • Tidy sofas and cushions

Bedroom

  • Make the bed
  • Change bed linens (if provided)
  • Sweep and mop floors
  • Dust furniture and surfaces
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs

Bathroom

  • Clean sink and countertop
  • Clean and disinfect toilet
  • Scrub shower and bathtub
  • Wipe mirrors and glass
  • Mop floors

Kitchen

  • Wipe countertops
  • Clean sink and faucet
  • Clean stove and microwave (outside)
  • Wipe cabinet surfaces
  • Sweep and mop floors
